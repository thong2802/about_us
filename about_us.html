About Us

Aug Leather is a startup that produces handcrafted leather goods.
Empowered by international E-commerces, our visions are to build up a strong, reliable branch name to provide the best-handcrafted products at an acceptable price.
Customer-first and only offer products we are proud of are our principles.

 

Premium Quality

We use the most endurable kinds of leather to make our watch straps, wallets, and other products. Every production stage has our quality control to ensure that no error or mistake can affect their quality. 

 

High Level Of Craftsmanship

As a team of experienced craftsmen, we share our passion for leather and put all our efforts into making every product. Each of our products is a unique combination of high craftsmanship and great enthusiasm. 

 

Customization

We acknowledge that every customer has his/ her characteristics and preferences. Therefore, AugLeather provides the full options to customize your items. Just tell us how you would like your item, we will build it up to suit you best. 

 

Best price

Price is the biggest that differentiates us from other luxury brands. With similarities in aesthetics and quality, we are proud to offer the most affordable prices. 

 

Customers stay the first on our priority list of all time.
Therefore, your comfort, satisfaction, and feedback are all highly valued!
